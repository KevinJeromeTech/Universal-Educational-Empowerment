Scholarship Programs

Removing financial barriers to open doors to brighter futures

About This Program

The UEE Scholarship Program provides financial assistance to students from underrepresented and underserved communities, removing the economic barriers that prevent talented individuals from accessing higher education and vocational training. We believe that financial hardship should never determine the limits of someone's future.

Available Scholarships

UEE Merit Scholarship

Awarded to students demonstrating academic excellence and a commitment to community service, regardless of financial need.

Need-Based Grant

Financial assistance for students who demonstrate significant financial need and have the drive to succeed academically.

Vocational Training Award

Support for students pursuing skilled trades and vocational programs that lead to stable, well-paying careers.

First-Generation Scholarship

Dedicated support for students who are the first in their family to pursue post-secondary education.

How to Apply

  1. Complete the online application form (opens each spring).
  2. Submit transcripts and two letters of recommendation.
  3. Write a 500-word personal statement on your educational goals.
  4. Attend a brief virtual interview with the scholarship committee.
  5. Award decisions are communicated within 6 weeks of the application deadline.
