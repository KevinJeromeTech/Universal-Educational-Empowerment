Youth Leadership & Empowerment

Equipping the next generation to lead with purpose

About This Program

The Youth Leadership & Empowerment program equips young people — ages 14 to 24 — with the leadership skills, confidence, and opportunities they need to drive meaningful change in their communities and beyond. Through hands-on experiences, mentorship, and structured leadership training, participants emerge ready to become the changemakers of tomorrow.

Program Highlights

Leadership Bootcamp

An intensive multi-week training in communication, decision-making, team management, and ethical leadership.

Community Projects

Youth design and execute real projects that address local challenges, building tangible impact and a portfolio of experience.

Public Speaking & Advocacy

Training and practice in presenting ideas, public speaking, and advocating for policy changes that matter to their communities.

Young Leaders Network

A peer network connecting youth leaders across regions to share ideas, collaborate, and support each other's growth.

Eligibility

Open to young people aged 14–24 from any background. Priority is given to youth from under-resourced communities, first-generation college aspirants, and those who demonstrate a passion for community service — even without formal experience.
