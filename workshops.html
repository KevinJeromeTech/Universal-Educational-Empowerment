Workshops & Training Programs

Practical skills for real-world career readiness

About This Program

Our Workshops & Training Programs bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world application. Whether you're entering the workforce for the first time, pivoting careers, or looking to sharpen existing skills, our workshops offer structured, expert-led training in a supportive environment — all at no cost to participants.

Workshop Categories

Professional Skills

Time management, workplace communication, team collaboration, and professional email and presentation skills.

Financial Literacy

Budgeting, saving, understanding credit, and planning for long-term financial health and stability.

Entrepreneurship Basics

How to start and run a small business — from idea validation and business planning to marketing and operations.

Health & Wellness

Workshops on mental health awareness, stress management, nutrition basics, and building sustainable healthy habits.

Format & Schedule

Workshops are held both in-person at community centers and virtually via video conference. Most sessions run 2–4 hours and are offered on weekends and evenings to accommodate working adults and students. New workshops are announced monthly — check back or sign up to be notified.
