Digital Skills Training

Preparing participants for a technology-driven world

About This Program

Digital fluency is no longer optional — it's essential. Our Digital Skills Training program offers a structured path from basic computer literacy all the way to advanced digital tools, giving every participant the foundation they need to thrive in a modern economy. No prior experience is required; every learner starts where they are.

Skill Tracks

Foundations Track

Computer basics, internet safety, email, word processing, and spreadsheets — the building blocks of digital life.

Productivity & Office Tools

Deep dives into tools like Google Workspace and Microsoft 365 for professional-level productivity and collaboration.

Web & Social Media

How to build a basic website, manage a professional online presence, and use social media for career and community goals.

Intro to Coding

An accessible introduction to programming concepts using beginner-friendly languages, laying groundwork for a tech career.

Equipment & Access

Participants who lack access to a computer or reliable internet connection can borrow devices through our lending program and attend in-person sessions at partner community centers equipped with computer labs. We remove barriers so that technology access is never an obstacle to learning.
