Career Development Training

Building the skills and confidence for long-term career success

About This Program

Our Career Development Training program is designed to help participants enter or advance in the workforce with confidence. From crafting a compelling resume to acing a job interview to navigating professional relationships, we provide the tools and coaching that turn potential into opportunity — especially for those who have faced systemic barriers to employment.

What's Included

Resume & Cover Letter Writing

One-on-one coaching to craft tailored, professional resumes and cover letters that stand out to employers.

Mock Interview Practice

Realistic interview simulations with detailed feedback to build confidence and sharpen responses.

Job Search Strategies

How to find job openings, use LinkedIn effectively, network professionally, and navigate hiring processes.

Workplace Readiness

Workplace culture, professional expectations, conflict resolution, and how to grow once you've landed the role.

Employer Partnerships

UEE works with a growing network of local and national employers committed to inclusive hiring. Program graduates gain access to our job placement assistance service and are connected directly with partner employers actively seeking diverse talent. Completion of the program comes with a UEE certificate recognized by our employer network.
